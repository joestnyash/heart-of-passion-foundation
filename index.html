Introduction

Heart of Passion Foundation is a nonprofit charitable organization based in Nairobi, Kenya, founded in 2016 by Reverend JoeFaye (Joseph Nyaga Gachoki).

We support vulnerable communities, empower GBV survivors, and care for children through our home by providing food, shelter, education, and emotional support.
