Introduction

Founded in 2016 by Reverend JoeFaye (Joseph Nyaga Gachoki), Heart of Passion Foundation supports vulnerable children and communities in Nairobi.

We provide shelter, education, food, and emotional care to children while empowering families and GBV survivors.
