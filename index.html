HEART OF PASSION FOUNDATION

Bonded in Love ♥️, Humanity, and By God

Founded in 2016 by Rev. JoeFaye (Joseph Nyaga Gachoki), we support vulnerable children, GBV victims and community outreach programs including prison ministry.

📖 Introduction

1. Founder

My name is Rev. JoeFaye (Joseph Nyaga Gachoki). I am a man whose life has been quietly but powerfully shaped by compassion, choosing service over self and purpose over comfort. Devoted to charity, I see beyond circumstances and recognize the dignity and worth in every person.

2. Children Ministry

We care for 42 children under Heart of Passion Children's Home providing Food, Shelter, Education, Clothing and love.

3. Prison Ministry

We visit prisons across Kenya providing Technical Skills, Communication Training, Paralegal support and essential items like toiletries, soaps, detergents, toothbrushes, footwear and innerwears.
