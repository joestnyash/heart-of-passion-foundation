Introduction

Founder: My Name Rev JoeFaye (Joseph Nyaga Gachoki). I am a man whose life has been quietly but powerfully shaped by compassion, choosing service over self and purpose over comfort. Devoted to charity, I see beyond circumstances and recognize the dignity and worth in every person.

Children Ministry: We care for 42 children under Heart of Passion Children's Home providing Food, Shelter, Education, Clothing and love.

Prison Ministry: We visit prisons across Kenya providing Technical Skills, Communication Training, Paralegal support and essential items like toiletries, soaps, toothbrushes and clothing.
