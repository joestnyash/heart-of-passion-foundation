Introduction

Founded in 2016 by Reverend JoeFaye (Joseph Nyaga Gachoki), Heart of Passion Foundation supports vulnerable children, GBV victims and families in Nairobi.

We run a children's home supporting over 42 children.

We also visit prisons and correctional institutions in Kenya where we provide guidance and counselling programs.

We provide essential items such as toiletries, bathing soaps, toothpaste, toothbrushes, towels and other needs not provided by the Government. The Government mainly provides food only.

We also run programs like Technical Training, Computer Courses and Communication Training to empower inmates.
