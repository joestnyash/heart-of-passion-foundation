About Us

Heart of Passion Foundation is a non-profit organization based in Nairobi, Kenya, dedicated to restoring hope, dignity, and purpose to vulnerable individuals and communities. We support the needy, empower survivors of gender-based violence, and run Heart of Passion Children’s Home, which provides care and shelter to 42 children.

Our Mission

To restore hope, dignity, and purpose to vulnerable individuals by providing compassionate care, protection, and long-term support through community outreach, GBV support programs, and children’s home services.

Our Vision

To build a society where every child is loved, every vulnerable person is protected, and every life has the opportunity to thrive.

Our Core Values

  • Compassion: We serve with love and empathy.
  • Integrity: We are transparent and accountable.
  • Dignity: Every life matters and deserves respect.
  • Commitment: We focus on long-term impact.
  • Empowerment: We uplift and restore independence.
  • Community: We believe in working together.
