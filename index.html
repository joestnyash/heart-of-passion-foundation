HEART OF PASSION FOUNDATION

Bonded in Love ♥️, Humanity, and By God

We are a nonprofit organization in Nairobi, Kenya founded in 2016 by Rev. JoeFaye (Joseph Nyaga Gachoki). We support vulnerable children, empower communities, and run outreach programs including prison ministry, education support, and humanitarian aid.

📖 Introduction

We care for children by providing food, shelter, education, and emotional support. We also visit prisons and correction facilities offering guidance, counseling, and essential items like toiletries and clothing.
