Introduction

Heart of Passion Foundation is a nonprofit charitable organization based in Nairobi, Kenya, founded in 2016 by Reverend JoeFaye (Joseph Nyaga Gachoki). The foundation is committed to restoring hope, dignity, and purpose to vulnerable communities.

Through our children’s home, community outreach, and support for GBV survivors, we provide food, shelter, education, and emotional care to those in need. Our mission is to transform lives and build a better future—because every life matters.
