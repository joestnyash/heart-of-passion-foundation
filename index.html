We care for 42 children providing shelter, food, education and love. We also visit Prisons offering counseling and essential supplies like Towels, Toiletries,Bathing and Washing Soaps,ToothBrushes and ToothPaste,Shoes. We also have Technical trainings E.g Computer Lessons, Communication Skills Training them to Express themselves and air out their Grievances and stand for their Rights.Seek Legal Advises and Mitigations. We also have Clinical Camps.We also Serve as a Bridge between Family and Inmates where we Link them through Family Visits and Communication Means
