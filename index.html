Our Mission

Heart of Passion Foundation exists to restore hope, dignity, and purpose to vulnerable individuals and communities across Kenya. We are committed to supporting the needy, empowering survivors of gender-based violence, and providing a safe, nurturing home for orphaned and abandoned children. Through compassion-driven action, we meet immediate needs while creating long-term transformation.

Our Vision

To build a society where every child is loved, every vulnerable person is protected, and every life is given the opportunity to thrive—regardless of their background or circumstances.

Our Core Values

Compassion: We serve with genuine love and empathy, putting the needs of the vulnerable first.

Integrity: We uphold transparency, accountability, and honesty in all our work.

Dignity: Every human being deserves respect, care, and a meaningful life.

Commitment: We are dedicated to creating lasting impact, not just temporary relief.

Empowerment: We uplift individuals and communities to regain independence and hope.

Community: We believe in working together with partners, donors, and volunteers to create sustainable change.

“When you support Heart of Passion Foundation, you are not just giving—you are becoming part of a life-changing story.”
