HEART OF PASSION FOUNDATION

Not only a cause, but where every Heart ♥️ Matters!

Heart of Passion Foundation is a nonprofit charitable organization based in Nairobi, Kenya, dedicated to transforming lives and restoring hope within vulnerable communities. Founded in 2016 by Reverend JoeFaye (Joseph Nyaga Gachoki), the foundation supports the needy, empowers GBV survivors, and runs a children’s home caring for 42 children.

We provide food, shelter, education, and emotional care—because every life matters.
