Introduction

Heart of Passion Foundation is a nonprofit organization founded in 2016 by Reverend JoeFaye (Joseph Nyaga Gachoki).

We support vulnerable communities, empower GBV survivors, and care for children through provision of food, shelter, education and emotional support.
