HEART OF PASSION FOUNDATION

Bonded in Love ♥️, Humanity, and By God

Founded in 2016 by Rev. JoeFaye (Joseph Nyaga Gachoki), we support vulnerable children, GBV victims and community outreach programs including prison ministry.

📖 Introduction

1. Founder

My name is Rev. JoeFaye (Joseph Nyaga Gachoki). I am a man whose life has been quietly but powerfully shaped by compassion, choosing service over self and purpose over comfort. Devoted to charity, I see beyond circumstances and recognize the dignity and worth in every person. My hands are always extended to lift others, bringing hope where there is despair, provision where there is lack, and light where there is darkness. I am not defined by what I have, but by what I give.

2. Children Ministry

We care for 42 children under Heart of Passion Children's Home. We provide Food, Shelter, Education, Clothing, and a loving environment where every child is nurtured, protected, and empowered to grow into a responsible and hopeful future.

3. Prison Ministry

We visit prisons across Kenya where we run programs that directly impact inmates. We provide Technical Skills such as Computer Lessons, Communication Skills to help inmates express themselves, Paralegal guidance and study materials, and support in developing career skills and talents.

We also provide essential personal items including Toiletries, Washing and Bathing Soaps, Detergents, Toothpaste and Toothbrushes, Footwear, Innerwears, and other necessities that are not provided by the government.
