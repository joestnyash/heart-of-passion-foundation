Introduction

Heart of Passion Foundation is a nonprofit charitable organization based in Nairobi, Kenya, founded in 2016 by Reverend JoeFaye (Joseph Nyaga Gachoki).

We are committed to transforming lives by supporting the needy, empowering GBV survivors, and caring for children through our children's home. We provide food, shelter, education, and emotional care—because every life matters.
