HEART OF PASSION FOUNDATION

Bonded in Love ♥️, Humanity, and By God

Founded in 2016 by Rev. JoeFaye (Joseph Nyaga Gachoki), we support vulnerable children, GBV victims and community outreach programs including prison ministry.

📖 Introduction

We care for 42 children providing shelter, food, education and love. We also visit prisons offering counseling and essential supplies.

Founder: He is a man whose life has been quietly but powerfully shaped by compassion, choosing service over self and purpose over comfort. Devoted to charity, he sees beyond circumstances and recognizes the dignity and worth in every person he encounters. His hands are always extended to lift others, his heart anchored in generosity, and his actions speak louder than words—bringing hope where there is despair, provision where there is lack, and light where there is darkness. He is not defined by what he has, but by what he gives, embodying the true spirit of selflessness and humanity.

While we visit Prisons in Kenya, we have Programs that directly impact the lives of inmates. We provide Technical Skills like Computer Lessons, Communication Skills that help inmates express themselves to authorities, offer Paralegal guidance and study materials, develop career skills, and nurture talents and artistic excellence.

We also provide essential personal items including Toiletries, Washing and Bathing Soaps, Detergents, Toothpaste and Toothbrushes, Footwear, Innerwears, and many other basic needs.
