Introduction

Heart of Passion Foundation is a nonprofit organization founded in 2016 by Reverend JoeFaye (Joseph Nyaga Gachoki), supporting vulnerable children, GBV survivors and families in Nairobi.

We provide shelter, education, food, and emotional care because every life matters.
