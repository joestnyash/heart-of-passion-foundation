Introduction

Founded in 2016 by Reverend JoeFaye (Joseph Nyaga Gachoki), Heart of Passion Foundation supports vulnerable children and communities.

We also visit prisons and correctional institutions in Kenya where we provide guidance and counselling programs.

We provide essential items such as toiletries, bathing soaps, toothpaste, toothbrushes, towels and other needs not provided by the Government.

The Kenyan Government mainly provides food only.

We also run programs like Technical Training, Computer Courses and Communication Training to empower inmates.
