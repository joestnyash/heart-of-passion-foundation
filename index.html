✨ Introduction

Founder: Heart of Passion Foundation was founded in 2016 by Rev. JoeFaye (Joseph Nyaga Gachoki). He is a man whose life has been quietly but powerfully shaped by compassion, choosing service over self and purpose over comfort. Devoted to charity, he sees beyond circumstances and recognizes the dignity and worth in every person he encounters.

“Bringing hope where there is despair, provision where there is lack, and light where there is darkness.”

His hands are always extended to lift others, his heart anchored in generosity, and his actions speak louder than words—embodying the true spirit of selflessness and humanity.

Rev. JoeFaye

👶 Children Ministry: We care for 42 children under Heart of Passion Children's Home by providing Food, Shelter, Education, Clothing, and Love. Every child is nurtured in a safe environment where they are given an opportunity to grow, learn and thrive.

🔗 Prison Ministry: Our prison outreach programs are transformational. We provide guidance and counselling, offer technical skills training such as computer studies, promote talent development, arts and crafting. We identify and nurture talents, equipping inmates with tools to rebuild their lives.

We act as mediators between the accused and the accuser, and serve as a bridge between inmates and their families. We also provide Bibles and reading journals to strengthen their relationship with God during their time in correction.

Luke 6:38 – “Give, and it will be given to you...”

Matthew 25:40 – “Whatever you did for one of the least of these... you did for me.”
